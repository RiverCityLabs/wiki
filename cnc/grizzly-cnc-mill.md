## Specifications

* Motor size: 1 HP
* Table size: 26-5/8" x 7-1/16"
* Longitudinal travel: 450mm \(17.71"\)
* Cross travel: 120mm \(4.72"\)
* T-Nut Size: 7/16"
* Spindle taper: R-8
* Number of speeds: Variable
* Max spindle to table distance: 13"
* Max spindle to column distance: 7-1/2"
